What is the hydraulic horsepower?
Hydraulic horsepower is the horse power equivalent of lifting a continous volume rate of fluid to the height in ft represented by the pressure involved.
How to calculate hydraulic horsepower?
To calculate hydraulic horsepower you should use the following formula:
hydraulic horse pwer = (P * Q) / 1714
where:
P = pressure
Q = flow rate
Why use hydraulic horsepower?
The hydraulic horsepower is used in order to estimate the operating requirements and it is also used in sizing equipment.
